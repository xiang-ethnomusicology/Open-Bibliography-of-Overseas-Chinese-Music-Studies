# Open Bibliography of Overseas Chinese Music Studies  
## An Open Access Bibliography of Global Research on Chinese Music (English Language)

Welcome!  
This database collects **author + title** information of *international* scholarship on Chinese music, including works published in:

- ethnomusicology  
- musicology  
- sound/media studies  
- China studies  
- anthropology   

All entries are **English-language**.

## â­ Project Description

The goal of this project is to create an **open access**, curated, searchable bibliography that helps scholars, students, and the public understand the global landscape of Chinese music studies.

It includes:
- published books  
- journal articles  
- dissertations (selected)  
- book chapters  
- conference proceedings (selected)
